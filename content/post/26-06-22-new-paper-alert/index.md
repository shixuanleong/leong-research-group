“El Agente Seguro: An Agent for Chemical Safety,” is now available as a preprint on [ChemRxiv](https://chemrxiv.org/doi/abs/10.26434/chemrxiv.15004378), co-led by Shi Xuan and Yeonghun Kang. This collaboration took place during Shi Xuan's postdoctoral research at the Matter Lab, and demonstrates an intelligent planning-oriented safety agent. As the lab becomes increasingly autonomous, safety management system must also evolve to integrate safety intelligence into the reasoning layer of self-driving laboratories.
